# AJiaSu Usage Gate

Before any real-account CI or production use, the service owner must retain written evidence that the intended internal enterprise use complies with the applicable AJiaSu license and service terms. The evidence must explicitly cover permitted concurrency, container execution, credential automation, and selected-node behavior.

If that evidence is absent or the applicable terms are restrictive, stop after the fake CLI contract and binary-integrity checks. Do not place personal credentials in CI or source control.

The approval record must identify the reviewer, review date, terms version, permitted scope, approved concurrency, and operational restrictions. Store the approval record in the organization's compliance system; keep only a non-secret reference to that record in this repository.
